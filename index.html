About:

We created Spooky Boogie as part of CS188 - Virtual Reality Game Development. Over the course of 10 weeks, we learned how to use Unreal Game Engine to create a game playable on the Oculus Rift. We used Blueprints and Unreal's experimental Environment Query System to create a realistic interactive environment and spooky AI ghosties.

Oculus Playthrough

Important:

We used Unreal Engine v4.7.6. Future versions may or may not affect the performance of this game.

Controls:

WASD : Move
SPACE: Jump
E: Pick up keys or open doors.
F: Toggle Flashlight. Flashlight slowly drains and charges when turned off.
R-CLICK: Toggle Camera
L-Click: Fire Camera - 10 second cooldown. Any spooky ghosts hit by camera flash are briefly stunned.
